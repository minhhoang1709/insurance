ACCOUNT

The 9Lives application is available for Android phone users with version 5.0.0 or higher and can be downloaded via the Google Play.

To sign up for 9Lives application you can simply use an active Google account.

You can add personal information via the top left button and open the user profile, please specify your full name according to your identity card, gender, date of birth, place of birth and mobile phone number.

Open the user profile and click change in the top right button, personal information that can be updated is only your telephone number.

INSURANCE PURCHASE

Please select the time period and type of compensation you need and press the start button, the amount of premium that must be paid will appear automatically adjusting to your choice and then press the next button. Make sure the contents of personal data correctly, because you will be asked to upload a photo of the front of the valid ID card.

The minimum amount of insurance purchases is IDR 5,000 (Five Thousand Rupiah).

9Lives insurance purchases only available for those over 17 years old

  • The maximum age limit for 9Lives personal accident insurance participants is 60 years.
  • The maximum age limit for 9Lives travel lite insurance participants is 75 years.

Children ages for 9Lives travel lite insurance participants is between 2 to 16 years.

PAYMENT

  1. Credit Card (we accept VISA, Master Card, and JCB).
  2. ATM Transfer (Bank Mandiri, Bank Permata, Bank BNI, ATM Bersama, Prima, or ALTO network).
  3. GO-PAY platform.

INSURANCE POLICY

To get your insurance policy document, please open my insurance menu then select your Policy button at the top right. The coverage document will be downloaded to your mobile in the form of a pdf file.

Through the settings menu and press the standard policy button.

CLAIM PROCESS

There are 6 stages in making insurance claims, the method is very easy and done online directly through the 9Lives application, open the My Insurance menu then select the active policy and press the claim button. Make sure you have prepared the information as follows:

  1. Accident Details
  2. Coverage List
  3. Upload documents (medical reports and hospital fee receipt)
  4. Your bank account information
  5. Statement of truth
  6. Final Confirmation

FREE INSURANCE

You are entitled to get free insurance compensation for 2 weeks accidental only emergency room benefit by:

  1. Making insurance purchase more than IDR 30,000 (Thirty Thousand Rupiah).
  2. Invite your friends to use 9Lives application with the invitation link.
  3. Additional coverage will be obtained after your friend is registered as a 9Lives insurance participant.
  4. Free promos are limited to 3 (three) times per user account.
  5. Free insurance applies to users and the owner of the invitation code.
  6. If you already have a policy with the same type of compensation in an active condition, the free insurance will be activated after the previous policy period ends.

Press the menu button, then choose Free Insurance. Enter the invitation code or company code, then press "Use" to get insurance for free.
This code can only be used by registered corporate participants with 9Lives.
